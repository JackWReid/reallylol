## Day Job
At my day job at Miro I’m an Engineering Manager. I work from the office basically every day.

I use my company-issued **MacBook Pro “14, 2022** (M1).

I really like handwritten notes but I hate writing them up. When I'm making notes by hand, I don't use a bound notebook any more. I use notepads with tear away pages. At the moment I'm writing my notes digitally, using the **Obsidian** markdown note editor knowledge-base thingy. One type of note I keep is a daily agenda that I write at the end of the previous day.It’s just headings of the projects I’m working on with a bullet pointed list of stuff to work through. Writing it at the end of the workday helps me empty my mind of work when I go home, and reading it in the morning feels like loading a condensed version of yesterday’s work brain again.

The apps I have open all day every day are **Firefox**, **Chrome**, **Mail**, **Slack**, **Ghostty**, and **Obsidian**. My passwords are all managed through **1Password** and some work logins are managed by IT through **Okta**. As of 2026, there's always a terminal window open with **Claude Code** up to no good in there.

I use **Fish** shell. I use **Neovim** or **Zed** for what little development I do. Towards the end of 2020 I moved into a more management heavy role, which means less time in Terminal and more time in **Obsidian** and **Google Docs**.

## Daily Carry
My backpack is a black Patagonia Black Hole 32L, though I also have a smaller black Patagonia backpack that I found on the street. My bike is a cheap Decathlon single-speed, which I have learned not to name.

I carry a **Green Kaweco Sport** fountain pen and I write in hardcover **Leuchturm 1917** A5 dotted notebooks.
