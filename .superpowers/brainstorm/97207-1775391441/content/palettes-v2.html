On walking through Ludlow in February

The high street curves and you lose sight of the castle for a moment. There's a butcher with hand-lettered signs and a bookshop that smells like it should.

You walk past the church and the light changes. The stone goes from grey to almost gold, and you understand why people have been living here for a thousand years.

1. Warm Stone

Linen and terracotta. Warm off-white, dark brown text, golden-brown accents. The closest to "ceramic in a bookshop". Cosy without being twee.
